Overview

Purpose

My purpose is to bring people together and share experiences through whitewater rafting. Through this website, people can access information and sechedule rafts easily and share with their friends. Doing this will increase sales and credibility within the community and further accomplish our purpose.

Audience

The type of people I expect to be looking at this site are young adults. I need to keep in mind that younger people are going to be visiting this site so it should look pleasing to the eye and be really simple with eye catching fonts.
